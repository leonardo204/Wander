# Wander App Icon Prompt

## 앱 아이덴티티

**Wander**는 여행 사진의 GPS와 시간 메타데이터를 분석하여 자동으로 타임라인을 생성하고, AI가 여행 스토리를 작성해주는 iOS 앱입니다.

**핵심 키워드**: 여행, 사진, 위치(GPS), 타임라인, AI 스토리텔링

---

## 아이콘 컨셉

```
W (Wander) = 여행 경로 + AI 스파클
```

"W"를 여행 경로처럼 표현하고, 작은 AI 스파클로 스토리 생성을 암시

### Prompt C-2

```
Design a modern iOS app icon for "Wander" - a travel photo app with AI storytelling.

CONCEPT:
The letter "W" stylized as a winding travel path, with a small sparkle at the end of the journey - representing AI transforming the trip into a story. The sparkle sits at the top-right end of the W path, like magic happening at the destination.

VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Abstract "W" shape designed to look like a flowing travel route or winding path
- Small sparkle (✦) at the top-right corner, positioned at the "end" of the W journey
- The sparkle should be subtle but noticeable - suggesting AI magic
- W flows from bottom-left upward to the sparkle

COLORS:
- Background: Sky Blue gradient (top #5DADE2 to bottom #87CEEB)
- W symbol: White (#FFFFFF)
- Sparkle: White, slightly glowing effect

STYLE:
- Modern 2024-2026 iOS app icon aesthetic
- Soft, rounded edges on the W letterform
- Fluid, organic curves suggesting movement and journey
- Minimal and premium feel
- Not playful or cartoonish

TECHNICAL:
- 1024x1024 pixels
- Square format
- PNG with gradient background
- Clean, crisp edges
- Professional App Store quality
```
